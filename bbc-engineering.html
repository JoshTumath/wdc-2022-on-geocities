Where is it?

OLD TRAFFORD

No, not the football one - the cricket one. The conference is taking place at the The Point, the conference centre in the world famous Emirates Old Trafford Cricket Ground in Manchester. It's an absolute nightmare to get to from MediaCityUK, but easy to get to from Manchester's main train stations.
